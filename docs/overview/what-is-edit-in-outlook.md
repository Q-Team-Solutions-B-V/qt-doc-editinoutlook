# What is Edit in Outlook?

Edit in Outlook is a powerful Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central app developed by Q-Team Solutions that provides seamless integration between Business Central and Microsoft Outlook for sending documents via email.

## Main Functionality

### Problem Being Solved
With standard Dynamics 365 Business Central, you can easily send sales & purchase documents as PDF or electronic documents via email to your customers, and create beautiful email templates in your company's style. However, with standard Business Central, you cannot send them with Outlook without adding your email address to Business Central.

### The Solution
Edit in Outlook solves this problem by:
- Sending directly from your own email address
- Using Business Central email templates
- Allowing email editing in Outlook before sending
- Storing emails in your personal Outlook archive

## Key Benefits

### 🚀 **Seamless Integration**
- Full integration between Business Central and Outlook
- No duplicate email address configuration required
- Maintains Business Central functionality

### 📧 **Enhanced Email Workflow**
- Edit emails in the familiar Outlook environment
- Easily add attachments
- Preview documents before sending
- Automatic storage in Outlook 'Sent Items'

### 👤 **Personal Email**
- Send from your own email address
- Maintain personal email signature
- Professional appearance to customers

### 📋 **Document Management**
- Supports all Business Central documents:
  - Sales & purchase quotes
  - Orders

  - And other documents
- PDF and electronic document support
