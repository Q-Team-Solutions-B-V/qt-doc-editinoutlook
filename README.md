Comprehensive documentation for the Q-Team Solutions Edit in Outlook app for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central.

## About Edit in Outlook

Edit in Outlook enables users to edit Business Central document email templates and send them via Microsoft Outlook. The app provides seamless integration between Business Central and Outlook for an enhanced user experience.

## Key Features

- **Send documents via Outlook**: Send sales & purchase documents directly from Business Central via Outlook
- **Edit email templates**: Use Business Central email templates and edit them in Outlook
- **Add attachments**: Easily add attachments to your emails
- **Personal email address**: Send from your own email address without adding it to Business Central
- **Storage in Outlook**: Emails are stored in Outlook's 'Sent Items'
